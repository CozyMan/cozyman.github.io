Dump Trailer



Product Description

The dump semi trailer, also known as a tipper trailer, is a heavy-duty vehicle designed to transport and dump loose materials such as sand, gravel, and construction waste.
It consists of a hydraulic system that allows the trailer bed to be raised at an angle, allowing the load to be discharged from the rear end.
The trailer is typically made of high-strength steel and can carry a payload of several tons. Dump semi trailers are widely used in the construction and mining industries and are an essential tool for transporting materials to and from worksites.
